About Us

Who are we?

Motor Rentals is a website that allows you to book any vehicle of your choice. Here at Motor Rentals we not only provide you with quality vehicles but we also provide on road assistance, just in case you need any. Motor Rentals allows you to book any vehicle without any hassle with the help of our Retail Sales Bot. You have to pay only for the services that you avail. No more, no less!

Motor Rentals is a trusted website with physical stores in various parts of the country. We have a wide range of vehicle collections both two wheelers and four wheelers, all you need to do is pick your ride and verify that you have a legal driving license.
